Our Story

Crafting Comfort & Elegance Since 1996

Durga Furniture has been a landmark of quality in Coimbatore. Founded in 1996 as a small wooden furniture workshop, we have grown into one of Coimbatore's premier manufacturers of luxury home wooden furniture and industrial steel furniture.

Every piece is designed to harmonize aesthetics, durability, and ergonomic support. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing factory utilizes seasonal treatment processes to make sure our wood lasts generation after generation.

Factory Profile

About Durga Furniture

Avinashi Road's Premier Furniture Pioneer

Founded in Coimbatore in 1996, Durga Furniture has set the standard for premium residential and commercial furniture. Operating our own integrated wooden seasoning and steel tube fabrication factories, we deliver highest durability directly to home owners and commercial offices.

Sustainable Teak Seasoning & Design Process

We utilize state of the art wood kiln seasoning chambers to bring moisture levels down to 8-12%, preventing splits and termite infestations. Our steel collection utilizes high-grade structural alloys treated with zinc phosphate coating layers to resist moisture oxidation.

Coimbatore Manufacturing Hub

With over 50 skilled carpenters, welders, and finishing specialists, our local workshop guarantees that every bed, sofa set, dining table, and steel cupboard meets rigorous quality tests before transport.
